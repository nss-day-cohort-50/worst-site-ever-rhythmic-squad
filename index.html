Lampray Pie

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • 1 pound eel
  • 1/2 C brown bread crumbs (1/3 C if blood is used)
  • 1/4 C wine vinegar (2T if blood is used)
  • 1/4 C fresh eel or fish blood (optional)
  • 1/4 C dry wine
  • 1/4 tsp. each cinnamon and black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 C sweet wine
  • 1/4 tsp. powdered ginger
  • 3 slices firm white bread
  • 1 T brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Combine bread crumbs, vinegar, dry wine, cinnamon, salt and pepper
  3. Place eels and sauce in crust
  4. Bake 45 minutes
